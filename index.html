Order Management is the operational core of Sales Finance — the team that validates, processes, and closes every deal flowing through Amplitude's sales motion. These tools were built specifically for OM workflows: catching booking errors before they become cases, managing the live queue, and getting new team members up to speed fast.

A step-by-step deal validation tool built from the OM booking checklist. Walk through each required field — Contract ID, ARR, deal type, billing terms — for Direct, Reseller, and AWS Marketplace deals. Flags issues before they reach the queue and become correction cases.

What is Work Bud?
Built for output,
not just activity.

Work Bud brings your Asana deliverables in-page with checkboxes you can actually use, keeps your meeting notes structured and searchable by week, logs the positive call-outs from teammates that belong in your performance record, and tracks every AI build in flight — all in one focused window, no tab-switching required.
