At a Glance · For the Uninitiated
What is Order Management?

Every deal Amplitude's sales team closes flows through Order Management before it's recognized as revenue. OM validates that deals are structured correctly — right ARR, right deal type, right billing terms — and processes any corrections before the deal books. When a booking is wrong, it creates a case. These tools exist so that doesn't happen.

What is Work Bud?
Built for output,
not just activity.

Work Bud surfaces your deliverables with checkboxes you can use right in the browser, keeps meeting notes structured and scannable by week, logs positive call-outs from teammates that belong in your performance record, and tracks every active build in flight — all in one focused window.
